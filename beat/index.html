How to use: Select an audio input source (microphone, system audio, or line-in), then click "Start Detection". Adjust the frequency ranges by dragging the colored rectangles' edges or middle. The yellow dashed lines are the detection thresholds - drag them up/down to adjust sensitivity (higher = less sensitive, lower = more sensitive). The threshold value shows how much above average the energy must be to trigger a beat (e.g., 1.3x = 30% above average).
